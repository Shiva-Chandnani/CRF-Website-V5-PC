We build clothes to your exact measure. That means we hold personal details you have trusted us with — and this notice sets out, in plain language, what we keep, why, and the rights you hold over it under Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

01 Introduction

Country Road Fashions is a bespoke tailoring house based in Bangkok. This notice explains how we handle your personal data across our website, our fitting rooms, and our order records. It applies whenever you browse, create an account, save measurements, or book a consultation with us.

02 Who we are

We are the data controller for the information described in this notice. You can reach us at our Bangkok atelier:

03 What we collect

  • Identity & contact — your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Account credentials — your email and a hashed password, held by our authentication provider. We never see your password.
  • Measurements & fit preferences — body measurements, reference-garment measurements, and any notes you choose to save.
  • Communication preferences — your newsletter opt-in status and the moment consent was given.
  • Consultation bookings — appointment details passed to our scheduling provider.
  • Cart contents — held in your browser only; nothing reaches us until you complete a purchase.
  • Technical data — authentication tokens kept in your browser's localStorage, plus standard server access logs.

04 Why we collect it

Each category rests on a lawful basis under the PDPA:

  • Contract performance — to take your measurements, fulfil your order, and deliver the garments you commission.
  • Consent — for newsletter communications and any optional marketing, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • Legitimate interest — to keep the service secure and to maintain accurate business records.

05 Who we share it with

We rely on a small set of sub-processors. We do not sell your data, and we never share it with advertisers.

  • Supabase — managed database and authentication. Your data is stored in the Singapore region.
  • Calendly — consultation scheduling, operated from the United States.

06 How long we keep it

  • Account & measurements — until you delete your account.
  • Order history — retained while your account is active. Tax-mandated invoice records may be held for the period required by the Thai Revenue Code, even after account deletion.
  • Newsletter subscription — until you unsubscribe.
  • Server logs — 90 days, on a rolling basis.

07 Your rights

Under the PDPA (sections 30–37) you may access your data, correct it, delete it, withdraw consent for marketing, and lodge a complaint with Thailand's Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC).

Once signed in, you can access, correct, and delete your data directly from your account page. To withdraw newsletter consent, use the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

08 Cookies & local storage

We do not use tracking cookies. Your sign-in session and your shopping cart are both stored in your browser's localStorage by the service itself. Because these are strictly necessary for the site to function, no consent banner is required.

09 Cross-border transfer

Your data is stored in Singapore by our database provider. The safeguards of PDPA section 28 apply to this transfer, supported by our provider's contractual commitments to an adequate standard of protection.

10 Changes to this notice

If we make a material change, we will email account holders before it takes effect, and the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised.

11 Contact us

For any request about your personal data — access, correction, deletion, or a complaint — please write to us at Countryroadfashions@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by the PDPA.
