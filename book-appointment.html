What to Expect

i.

Conversation, first

We start by understanding the occasion, your wardrobe, and how you want the garment to feel. No measurements until we agree on intent.

ii.

Cloth and cut

Bolts of Italian wool, Thai silk, and tropical linen are pulled. We narrow choices based on climate, formality, and how you'll wear it.

iii.

Measurements and timing

Accurate body measurements, posture notes, and a transparent production timeline — typically four to six weeks, with shipping included.
