# Country Road Fashions — Business & Brand Summary

## Business Overview

Country Road Fashions is a bespoke tailoring company and formalwear consultancy based in Bangkok, Thailand. The business presents itself as a heritage tailoring house with over 70 years of experience, combining traditional craftsmanship with a modern, personalized tailoring experience.

The brand serves both men and women, offering custom garments made to individual measurements and style preferences. Its core promise is centered on fit, personal expression, quality craftsmanship, and attentive customer service.

## Origin & Heritage

Country Road Fashions traces its origin back to 1951, when Tourmal Chandnani established a modest tailoring shop focused on crafting quality suits and officewear. The business built its early reputation through reliable products and strong customer service.

In 1992, the second generation continued the family tailoring tradition by opening a bespoke tailor shop in Bangkok, further strengthening the company’s reputation for service, fit, and client satisfaction.

By 2024, the third generation had modernized the business while preserving its tailoring legacy. This modernization includes digital tools such as online 3D suit customization, allowing clients to visualize and customize suits remotely while still grounding the brand in traditional craftsmanship.

## Location & Production

Country Road Fashions is based in Bangkok, Thailand. The company states that garments are made in its workshops in Bangkok. The business also notes that it is established in Thailand and has no local or overseas affiliates or branches.

The Bangkok location is central to the brand identity. The company positions itself within Thailand’s wider tailoring heritage, emphasizing Bangkok’s reputation for detailed craftsmanship, personalized service, competitive value, and international appeal.

## Core Brand Positioning

Country Road Fashions positions itself as a bespoke tailoring house that blends:

- Thai tailoring heritage
- Modern precision
- Premium fabric selection
- Personalized consultation
- Handmade craftsmanship
- International client service
- Accessible luxury compared with traditional high-end bespoke markets

The brand message is not simply about selling clothes. It frames tailoring as an experience: a process of creating garments that reflect the client’s individuality, confidence, lifestyle, and personal taste.

## Brand Philosophy

The central philosophy of Country Road Fashions is that clothing should be made for the individual. The brand repeatedly emphasizes that bespoke garments are created according to each client’s measurements, preferences, proportions, and intended use.

A key line from the website is: **“We stock only one size — yours.”**

This captures the brand’s focus on personal fit, individuality, and rejection of standard off-the-rack sizing.

## Client Experience

Country Road Fashions presents its tailoring process as private, guided, and highly personalized. Clients are invited to book consultations, where stylists or consultants help them choose fabrics, colours, styling details, and garments suited to their lifestyle or occasion.

The experience is described as relaxed but refined. The brand wants customers to feel guided rather than overwhelmed, with consultants helping translate personal preferences into finished garments.

The company also supports remote and international clients through online measurement submission, full-body photos, digital customization, global shipping, and trunk shows.

## Craftsmanship

Craftsmanship is one of the strongest brand pillars. Country Road Fashions describes its tailors as artisans with decades of experience. The website emphasizes that garments are cut, stitched, and finished by hand, with attention to fit, durability, structure, and detail.

Signature craftsmanship details mentioned include:

The brand connects craftsmanship with confidence, stating that a well-tailored suit is more than clothing; it is an expression of personality and enduring style.

Fabric guidance is part of the company’s service promise. Consultants help customers choose cloth based on climate, occasion, comfort, lifestyle, and desired level of formality.

## Customer Promise

Country Road Fashions’ customer promise can be summarized as:

1. **Personal fit** — garments are made to the client’s measurements, not standard sizing.
2. **Personal expression** — each piece should reflect the client’s identity, style, and occasion.
3. **Craftsmanship** — each garment is made with careful construction and finishing.
4. **Service** — clients are guided through fabric, fit, design, and delivery.
5. **Global access** — customers can order from Bangkok, online, or through trunk shows.
6. **Long-term satisfaction** — while full refunds are not offered due to the custom nature of the garments, the company states that it works with clients until they are satisfied with the final product.

## Refund & Satisfaction Policy Positioning

Because garments are made to each client’s size and style, Country Road Fashions states that full refunds are not feasible. However, the brand frames this policy around commitment to satisfaction, saying it will collaborate with customers until they are content with the final product.

This reinforces a service-led bespoke positioning: the product is custom and personal, so the solution to issues is adjustment, correction, and continued support rather than standard retail returns.

## Global & Remote Service

Country Road Fashions offers worldwide shipping for garments. The brand also supports remote ordering through online measurement guides and requests full-body photos from the front, side, and back when clients submit their own measurements.

The company’s international service includes trunk shows, where stylists meet clients in different cities. The trunk show process includes scheduling a meeting, meeting at a hotel or client’s home/office, taking measurements, reviewing fabric selections, designing the garments, producing the order in Bangkok, and shipping the finished garments to the client.

Trunk show garments are described as being manufactured and shipped within approximately 4–6 weeks, with expedited service available upon request. Another trunk show page notes an estimated 5–7 weeks to complete and ship bespoke orders, with quality checks before delivery.

## Trunk Shows

Country Road Fashions uses trunk shows as a way to bring its Bangkok bespoke experience to international clients. The brand mentions locations such as Singapore, the United States, Europe, New York, Madrid, and Singapore in its trunk show content.

The trunk show experience includes:

- Body measurements

This supports the brand’s image as both local and global: a Bangkok tailoring house with international reach.
